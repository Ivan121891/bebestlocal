Your first 30 days

What actually happens.

A week-by-week look at how we get from signed contract to live campaigns.

  • Week 1 — Kickoff

    Deep-dive audit

    2-hour strategy session. We connect to your ad accounts, CRM, analytics, and site. You meet your Success Manager and we agree on KPIs.

  • Week 2 — Strategy

    Growth blueprint delivered

    You receive a written plan: channels, offer positioning, creative direction, funnel structure, and 90-day targets. We lock scope together.

  • Week 3 — Build

    Assets in production

    Design begins on landing pages, ad creative, email sequences, and automation. You review drafts in a shared workspace, not a long email thread.

  • Week 4 — Launch

    Go live

    Campaigns launch. Tracking and dashboards go live. Daily monitoring starts. You get your first weekly performance report by Friday.

Ongoing cadence

How we stay aligned.

Weekly performance report

Every Friday you get a written report covering ad spend, cost per lead, booked appointments, revenue influenced, and what we're testing next week.

Monthly strategy call

A 45-minute call with your Success Manager to review the month, adjust priorities, and approve the next batch of creative and offers.

Quarterly business review

Deep retrospective every 90 days. What worked, what didn't, where to invest next. You leave with a refreshed roadmap.

Shared workspace 24/7

A dedicated Slack channel and Notion space. You can see deliverables, leave comments, and get replies in hours — not days.
